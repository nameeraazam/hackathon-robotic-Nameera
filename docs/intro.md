# Introduction to Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the Humanoid Robotics and Physical AI course. This comprehensive guide covers everything from the fundamentals of Physical AI to advanced humanoid systems, computer vision, and the latest SOTA (State of the Art) developments in the field.

## What You Will Learn

In this book, we explore:
- **Physical AI Foundations:** Understanding how AI interacts with the physical world.
- **Robot Description & Simulation:** Working with URDF, ROS 2, Gazebo, and NVIDIA Isaac Sim.
- **Perception & Vision:** Implementing Vision-Language Models (VLMs) and active perception.
- **Humanoid Project:** Building and simulating humanoid robots.
- **Ethics & Applications:** Discussing the societal impact and real-world uses of robotics.
